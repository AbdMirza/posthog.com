Message templates in PostHog enable you to create reusable content for your messaging campaigns. This helps maintain consistency across your communications and saves time when creating new messages.

## Creating a template

To create a message template:

1. Go to **Messaging** > **Templates** in the sidebar
2. Click **New template**
3. Give your template a name and add your content
4. Click **Create**

Templates support [Liquid templating](/docs/messaging/liquid-templating) for personalization.

## Using templates

You can use templates when creating messages in:

- [Campaigns](/docs/messaging/campaigns)
- [Broadcasts](/docs/messaging/broadcasts)

After you create your first template, a dropdown menu will appear when configuring a broadcast or campaign message. Selecting a template from the dropdown will apply it, and you can then customize the content as needed for that specific message.

## Best practices

- **Test your templates**: Always preview messages before sending to ensure variables render correctly
- **Maintain a consistent style**: Use templates to ensure brand consistency across all communications
- **Document your templates**: Add clear descriptions to help team members understand when to use each template
